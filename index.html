PM Prompts

Prime Your PM Skills with Inspiring Prompts


  • Product Requirements Document
    Generate a Product Requirement Document, including an outline of the key user problems or needs that this feature aims to address, along with a brief description of the proposed solution and its benefits.


  • Testing document
    Create a comprehensive testing checklist that includes a detailed breakdown of features, test cases, and expected results, ensuring thorough coverage of different scenarios and edge cases, while also providing clear instructions for setting up the testing environment


  • Product Notifcation
    Compose a concise and engaging product email that introduces our new feature, highlights its functionality, and explains how it improves the user experience, using persuasive language to encourage users to try it out.


  • Customer Pain Points
    Imagine you are a customer using our product. What challenges might you face and how could our product better address these?


  • Competitor Analysis
    Describe the key features and strengths of our top three competitors' products.


  • Brainstorming
    Generate innovative ideas for improving customer engagement with our product


  • Product Roadmap
    Suggest a product roadmap for the next two quarters based on the identified market needs and trends.


  • Go-to-Market Strategy
    Provide a high-level go-to-market strategy for our new feature release


  • Product Messaging
    Draft a compelling product announcement for our upcoming feature release.
